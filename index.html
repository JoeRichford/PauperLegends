Welcome to Pauper legends

Pauper Legends is a thrilling twist on the beloved Duel Commander format delivering the same intense 1v1 strategy, commander-focused gameplay, and high-stakes decision making, but with a refreshingly accessible twist. Built on a foundation of common-rarity cards and helmed by uncommon legendary creatures, Pauper Legends preserves the essence of competitive Duel Commander while slashing the entry cost. Whether you're a seasoned duelist or a newcomer eager to command your first army, this format invites everyone to experience powerful plays, clever synergies, and legendary battles all without breaking the bank. Prepare to duel smart, build creatively, and prove that greatness doesn't need a high price tag.

Rules

Pauper Duel Commander uses a special format:

  • Your deck is led by 1 Uncommon Legendary Creature – that’s your Commander.
  • Build a 99-card singleton deck – no repeats, except basic lands.
  • Use only cards printed at common rarity (from paper or digital sets) in your main deck.
  • Start at 20 life – just like regular Duel Commander.
  • London Mulligan rules are in effect – mulligan smart!
  • The player who goes first doesn’t draw on their first turn – keeps things fair.
  • Only one Commander is allowed to be cast from the command zone per game. - applies for both Partner or Backgrounds.

Banned & Restrictions

To keep gameplay fun, fair, and strategic, the following cards are currently banned:

  • Gut, True Soul Zealot. - proven problematic in a "common rarity" card pool setting.

Additional restrictions may apply to cards enabling infinite combos. Stay tuned for regular updates.
